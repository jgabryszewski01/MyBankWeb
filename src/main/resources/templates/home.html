Security

1. Protect Your Passwords

Never share your passwords with anyone, including bank employees. Choose strong passwords that are unique and difficult to guess. Remember to change your passwords regularly.

2. Be Cautious of Suspicious Links

Avoid clicking on links in emails or messages that seem suspicious or are from unknown sources. Always type the bank's URL directly into your browser or use trusted bookmarks.

3. Safeguard Personal Information

Never provide your personal information, such as Social Security number or PIN, in response to unsolicited requests. Legitimate organizations will not ask for sensitive information via email or phone.
